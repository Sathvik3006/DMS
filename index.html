About Us

Welcome to Department of IT

The Department of Information Technology has conceptualized in the year 2007. The Department has started a Program in B. Tech Information Technology with an intake of 60. The Department has been accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA in Tier-1) in 2015. By virtue of it, the intake has been enhanced from 60 to 120 with effect from the academic year 2016-17. The Department has been re-accredited by NBA in 2018 (in Tier-1). The intake has further enhanced to 180 from 2019-20. The Department has been re-accredited by NBA valid upto 30.06.2022

The key objective of department is to impact cutting-edge software education to students to meet challenging demands of the IT industry. Through this program, students equip with emerging technology developments and master in latest skills required by the Industry and Society at large.
